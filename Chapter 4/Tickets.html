Tickets

Get ready for an unforgettable experience at Digi Fest! This year, we’re celebrating collaboration and showcasing the latest digital innovations in Cardiff. Our programme is packed with educational talks, workshops, and inspiring showcases for all ages and interests.

Don’t miss out on the chance to explore cutting-edge technology and discover the exciting collaborations shaping the future. Whether you're a tech enthusiast, professional, or curious about the digital world, there’s something for everyone!

Ticket Options:
  • General Admission: Access to all talks, workshops, and showcases throughout Digi Fest.
  • VIP Pass: Enjoy exclusive access to VIP sessions, networking events, and special behind-the-scenes content.
  • Student/Youth Ticket: Discounted tickets for students and young attendees, offering access to all standard events.
Pricing:
  • General Admission: £20
  • VIP Pass: £50
  • Student/Youth Ticket: £10
